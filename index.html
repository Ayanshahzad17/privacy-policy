Privacy Policy

This app is designed to help users make informed choices about products by scanning their barcodes and checking product affiliations through public sources and APIs.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information such as name, email, phone number, contacts, or location. The app only uses the camera to scan barcodes.

Permissions

The app requires the following permission:

  • Camera: Used only to scan barcodes.
  • Internet Access: Required to fetch product information from public sources.

Children's Privacy

This app is not specifically targeted at children under the age of 13.

Third-party Services

The app may connect to third-party APIs and websites to retrieve publicly available product information. We do not control or store data from these services.
