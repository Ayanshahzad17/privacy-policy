Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

Thank you for using our Boycott Product Scanner app ("we," "our," or "the app"). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use the app.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information such as your name, email, phone number, location, contacts, or any other personal data.

2. Features and Permissions

  • Barcode scanning: The app uses your device’s camera to scan product barcodes.

3. How We Use Information

Since we do not collect personal data, we only use the scanned barcode data to fetch product information from online sources. No personal data leaves your device.

4. Children’s Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

5. Data Security

We do our best to keep your data safe, but since we do not collect or store any personal information, the risk is minimal.
